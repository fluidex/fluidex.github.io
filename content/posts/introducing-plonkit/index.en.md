## Functionalities
The functionalities of plonkit include:

+ Local Structured-Reference-String (SRS) setup
+ Verification Key Generation (corresponding to a given circuit)
+ Proof Generation
+ Proof Verfication
+ Verifier Smart Contract Generation
+ Proof Aggregation
